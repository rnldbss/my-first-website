The Salon

We are a Brazilian hairdresser salon near Chancery Lane and Holborn.
The salon was founded in Brazil, São Paulo, in 2000, where it was based for 16 years.
Nowadays we are based in Central London.
You’ll find our salon on Gray’s Inn Road, one of London’s Inns of Court since the 15th century, and a historic street connecting Chancery Lane station to King’s Cross. We are surrounded by a huge number of coffee shops, pubs and minutes walking from Tottenham Court Road and Covent Garden.
