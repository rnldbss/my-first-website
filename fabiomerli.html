Fabio Merli

Fabio Merli is our founder, he is 38 years old and 24 of these years are dedicated to the hairdressing industry.
He started his career at 14 years old working in his dad’s salon in São Paulo, Brazil. He worked for some great salons in São Paulo, Brazil, some examples are Glam Salon, Beleza Brasil and Jolis Salon. At the age 18 years old, he opened his first salon called Trend Hair Studio.
Over the years his clientele increased a lot, so he decided to open a bigger salon, called Studio Fascinação.
Fabio’s Salon was referenced by the beautiful blond hairs. He won in Brazil the Oscar Projeção Nacional and Tesoura de Ouro (Golden Scissor).
He moved from Brazil to London in 2016 and within just one year and 8 months in London he conquered his own space in Holland Park, one of the most popular neighborhoods in London, where he received people from all over the world and in the meantime he gave some masterclasses in Kuwait. The salon was based in Holland Park for about 3 years, and after this time, he conquered a bigger salon in Central London, where is currently based.
